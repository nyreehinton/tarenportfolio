Disney+

Black Stories Always

I developed the marketing strategy for Disney+'s Black History initiative, Black Stories Always - hailed by Forbes as "a win" . Black Stories Always reinforced The Walt Disney Company's continued commitment to diverse voices and stories.


Based on audience research, it was key that Disney+’s Black Stories Always campaign put black talent, culture and excellence at the forefront.With this campaign, Disney+ invited subscribers to celebrate the next generation of stories with a spotlight on Black storytelling, creators, culture, excellence and joy through the empowerment of diverse black voices. The campaign was anchored by the month’s biggest launches, including Black Panther: Wakanda Forever, which became the service's most watched Marvel global premiere on Disney+ to date ( Deadline , 2023).


The campaign came to life through international, social, digital, publicity, and synergy channels, including branded integrations promoting the campaign across Disney Parks and Resorts. It was also supported via Lifecycle channels, through a set of dedicated emails, and via a custom influencer campaign. On-service, the campaign was backed by two collection, including the refreshed Celebrate Black Stories Collection and all-new Black History Collection.

Warner Bros. Discovery

Say Yes to the Dress America

The fan-favorite TLC series, Say Yes to the Dress rang in New Year's 2020 with a twist. In S ay Yes to the Dress America, TLC celebrated brides from across the United States, sharing their journey as they traveled to Kleinfelds in search of their dream wedding dress. The season culminated with a two-hour wedding extravaganza involving America’s brides showing their state pride and hosted by TLC in NYC.


The goal of this campaign was to re-introduce audiences to the established series - breathing fresh air into a network staple. In order to achieve this , it was important to develop a comprehensive marketing campaign inclusive of multiple touch points including on-air, paid social, digital, radio, print, OOH and more. Partnerships with well-known brands in the wedding space were also crucial to campaign success. From a custom social partnership with registry staple, MyRegistry, to a unique wedding suite design collaboration with Appy Couple, this campaign helped celebrate a major moment in an established franchise's decade-long history.
